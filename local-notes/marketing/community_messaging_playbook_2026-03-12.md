## 2. Reddit FAQ / 常见质疑回复

### Q1: Is the data up to date?
**A:** Yes. We cover through the latest fiscal year currently available in the public DOL LCA disclosure data. The database is refreshed as new DOL releases become available.

### Q2: Why is this tool free/open source?
**A:** H-1B data should be easier to use, not locked behind expensive reports. H1B Finder is built to help job seekers make better decisions with transparent public data.

### Q3: How does the AI Assistant work? Is it hallucinating?
**A:** The assistant is grounded on structured H1B data slices such as company totals, salary ranges, and title-level patterns. If the relevant data is not available, it should say so instead of guessing.

### Q4: Does the site track me or sell my data?
**A:** The product should avoid unnecessary data collection. If chat logs are stored for quality improvement, that should be disclosed clearly and handled with retention limits and privacy safeguards.

### Q5: I found a salary that looks weird. Why?
**A:** Public filing data can contain format differences and occasional anomalies. Wages may also be annualized from hourly or other pay periods for comparison, so context matters.

### Q6: Can I use this for legal advice?
**A:** No. H1B Finder is a data and research tool, not legal advice. Users should consult a qualified immigration attorney for legal questions.

### Q7: The site is slow/down.
**A:** If traffic spikes, performance may temporarily degrade. A short acknowledgment plus status update is better than defensiveness.
