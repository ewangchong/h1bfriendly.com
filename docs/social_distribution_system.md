## 2. Content Templates (Reusable Formats)

### Template 1 — Top Sponsor Ranking Card
> 🏆 Top 10 H1B Sponsors for [ROLE] in [YEAR]
>
> 1. [Company] — [X] approvals
> 2. ...
>
> Full ranking → h1bfinder.com/companies
> #H1B #immigration #jobsearch

### Template 2 — Company Spotlight
> 📊 [COMPANY] sponsored [N] H1B roles in FY[YEAR].
> Top roles: [Title 1], [Title 2], [Title 3]
> Approval rate: [X]%
>
> See full profile → h1bfinder.com/companies/[slug]
> #H1Bvisa #techcareers

### Template 3 — Data Thread (X only)
> 🧵 Thread: What the FY[YEAR] H1B data tells us about [TREND]
>
> 1/ Total filings hit [N], [up/down] [X]% YoY
> 2/ [Observation about top industries]
> 3/ Fastest-growing sponsor: [Company]
> 4/ Average salary for [Role]: $[X]
> 5/ Full data → h1bfinder.com

### Template 4 — Trend Chart Visual
> 📈 H1B approvals by year for [ROLE/INDUSTRY]
> [Attach chart screenshot from /titles or /companies page]
>
> What trends do you see?
> Explore more → h1bfinder.com/titles

### Template 5 — Founder Commentary / Education
> As someone who went through the H1B process, here's what I wish I knew:
>
> 1. [Insight from data]
> 2. [Non-obvious strategy]
> 3. [Common mistake to avoid]
>
> Built h1bfinder.com to help others navigate this.

### Template 6 — State / City Spotlight
> 🗺️ Which states had the most H1B approvals in FY[YEAR]?
>
> 1. California — [N]
> 2. Texas — [N]
> 3. New York — [N]
>
> Find sponsors near you → h1bfinder.com/states

### Template 7 — Day-In-The-Life / User Story
> "I used h1bfinder.com to find that [Company] had a [X]% approval rate for [Role].
> Applied, got sponsored, and now I'm working in [City]."
>
> Your story could be next → h1bfinder.com/plan

### Template 8 — Weekly Recap
> This week on H1B Finder:
> ✅ [Feature shipped]
> 📊 [Interesting data point]
> 🔥 Most searched role: [Title]
>
> What should we build next?

## 6. Voice & Tone Guidelines

**DO:**
- Sound data-backed and confident
- Share real numbers from the platform
- Be helpful and educational, not promotional
- Use the founder's authentic voice

**DON'T:**
- Sound like a generic startup account
- Post without data or insight
- Use clickbait without substance
- Ignore comments or questions
